## 2. Description of the service

PadelRank is a free app that gives access to padel rankings, tournaments and club information across 18 federation-backed countries (Denmark, Sweden, Germany, Austria, Croatia, Norway, Romania, Slovenia, Moldova, Hungary, Finland, Ukraine, Estonia, Spain, Mexico, Argentina, Italy, France) and a Danish "Find a Match" community. The Tournaments tab additionally offers an **optional Playtomic Liga mode** that lists amateur club tournaments in **60+ countries** worldwide (off by default; toggled from "Choose Liga").
