2. Description of the service

PadelRanks is a free app that gives access to padel rankings, tournaments and club information across 18 federation-backed countries (Denmark, Sweden, Germany, Austria, Croatia, Norway, Romania, Slovenia, Moldova, Hungary, Finland, Ukraine, Estonia, Spain, Mexico, Argentina, Italy, France) and a Danish "Find a Match" community. The Tournaments tab additionally offers an optional Playtomic Liga mode that lists amateur club tournaments in 60+ countries worldwide (off by default; toggled from "Choose Liga").

PadelRanks reads from three independent public data sources: Rankedin (rankedin.com), FIP (padelfip.com) and Playtomic (playtomic.io). PadelRanks is independent of all three and is not an official product of any of them.

The Find a Match feature uses Supabase (supabase.com) to store user accounts, posts, comments and "I'm in" joins. PadelRanks is not affiliated with Supabase, Apple or Google beyond using their published services.
