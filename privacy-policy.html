PadelRank Privacy Policy

Zero analytics. Zero ads. Zero tracking.

Effective date: 6 May 2026
Provider: PadelRank

PadelRank is an iOS and Android app that gives padel players access to national federation rankings, international pro rankings, tournaments, team leagues and club information. Three independent data sources cover three different layers of the sport:

National federation rankings (Rankedin) — 13 countries:
Denmark, Sweden, Germany, Austria, Croatia, Norway, Romania, Slovenia, Moldova, Hungary, Finland, Ukraine, Estonia.
International pro rankings + Premier Padel / FIP Tour calendar (FIP):
Global, with deep player profile coverage for Spain, Mexico, Argentina, Italy and France.
Amateur club-tournament discovery (Playtomic) — 60+ countries:
Including all of Western Europe, the Nordics, Central & Eastern Europe, the Mediterranean, the Middle East, North & Latin America, Africa and Asia-Pacific. The Playtomic feed lights up wherever clubs publish their fixtures.

We take your privacy seriously and only process the data strictly required for the app to function.

1. Data we process

1.1 Location data

If you grant permission, PadelRank uses your location exclusively on your device to calculate the distance between you and padel tournaments and clubs. Your location is never sent to our servers. Outgoing requests to third-party tournament providers (see §3) only include coordinates when you explicitly run a location-based search — and only as anonymous latitude/longitude, never your identity. If you deny the location permission, the app still works — distance simply isn't shown.

1.2 No login, no account, no credentials

PadelRank does not require you to log in or create an account. The app uses public read-only data from third-party providers (see §3) to surface rankings, tournaments and player profiles. You can optionally pick your own player from the public Rankedin search to "claim" your profile in the app — that selection is stored only on your device and does not require credentials. We do not support logging in with Rankedin, FIP, or Playtomic accounts.

1.3 Third-party API data (rankings, tournaments, clubs, profiles)

The app fetches data from the public APIs listed in §3 to display rankings, tournament results and club information. This data is stored only temporarily in the app's on-device cache (AsyncStorage) to make the app faster; it is never forwarded anywhere.

1.4 Profile picture (optional)

If you choose to add a profile picture from your camera or photo library, it is stored only locally on your device.

1.5 Player profile photos displayed in the app

Profile photos of other players shown in rankings and profile screens are sourced from the public APIs of Rankedin and the International Padel Federation (FIP) — they are not hosted by us. Photo availability varies by player and by country; where a photo is not available we display a generated avatar with the player's initials. The image URLs themselves are requested directly from the third-party CDN by your device and are cached on-device for performance. No personal identifier is attached to those requests.

2. Data we do not collect

  • We have no analytics (no Google Analytics, Firebase, Facebook SDK, Mixpanel, etc.).
  • We show no ads.
  • We run no servers that log your usage.
  • We do not sell or share data with any third party.
  • We do not track you across apps or websites.

3. Third-party data sources

PadelRank communicates with three public data sources to show padel data. When you use the app, requests are sent directly from your device to these services — not through any server we operate. Each provider's own privacy policy applies to the data they process.

  • Rankedin — national rankings, tournaments, team leagues, player profiles and (optional) user login for the 13 Rankedin-backed countries listed above. Public endpoints on api.rankedin.com and the Azure Front Door CDN that serves profile thumbnails (rankedin-prod-cdn-adavg8d3dwfegkbd.z01.azurefd.net). Rankedin privacy policy: rankedin.com/privacy
  • FIP (Federación Internacional de Pádel) — Premier Padel + FIP Tour calendar, international pro rankings, recent finals, tournament breakdowns and player profile pages. Public WordPress REST API at padelfip.com/wp-json/fip/v1/... plus the public player pages at padelfip.com/player/{slug}/. FIP privacy policy: padelfip.com/privacy
  • Playtomic — amateur club-tournament discovery across 60+ countries covering Western Europe, the Nordics, Central & Eastern Europe, the Mediterranean, the Middle East, Africa, North & Latin America and Asia-Pacific. Public api.playtomic.io/v1/tournaments endpoint with coordinate-based geographic seed queries. Playtomic privacy policy: playtomic.io/privacy

Profile photos specifically come from:

  • Rankedin's Azure Front Door CDN (rankedin-prod-cdn-adavg8d3dwfegkbd.z01.azurefd.net/images/upload/player/{id}thumb.png) for the 13 Rankedin-backed countries;
  • FIP's WordPress Media library (padelfip.com/wp-content/uploads/…) for international pro players and the FIP-backed markets (Spain, Mexico, Argentina, Italy, France).

No crash reporting, analytics or telemetry SDK is currently integrated into the app. Should we later enable crash reporting (via Sentry), only anonymised technical details (stack trace, device model, OS version) would be sent — never personal data, location or credentials — and this section of the policy will be updated before such a change takes effect.

4. Your rights (GDPR + UK GDPR + Swiss DPA)

You have the right to:

  • Access — all your data lives on your device; you already have it.
  • Deletion — uninstall the app and all local data is wiped.
  • Portability — you can export your Rankedin data directly from rankedin.com.
  • Complaint — you can file a complaint with your national Data Protection Authority (e.g. Datatilsynet in Denmark, IMY in Sweden, BfDI in Germany, DSB in Austria, AEPD in Spain, Garante in Italy, CNIL in France, ICO in the UK) if you believe your rights have been infringed.

5. Children

PadelRank is suitable for users aged 6 and up — there's no chat, no in-app purchases, no user-generated content, and no advertising. We do not knowingly collect personal data about anyone, regardless of age. The app reads public sport data and stores user preferences (followed players, country selection) only on the device.

6. Changes

Changes to this policy will be published on this page together with an updated effective date. Material changes will be announced in the app.
